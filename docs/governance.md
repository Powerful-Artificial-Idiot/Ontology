# Governance

## Roles

- Domain owner: confirms business meaning and evidence.
- Ontology steward: owns term identity, definitions, reuse, and deprecation.
- Data steward: owns source mapping, quality, and provenance.
- Product owner: owns validated Demo behavior and presentation scope.
- Platform owner: owns CI, release integrity, runtime security, and observability.

## Change Gate

An ontology change requires a business question, definition, source evidence, namespace owner, compatibility assessment, SHACL impact, mapping impact, Demo alignment, competency query, and migration note where applicable.

## Knowledge States

- authoritative: asserted by an approved source or steward;
- derived: generated by a deterministic governed rule;
- candidate: proposed by search, extraction, or AI and awaiting review;
- deprecated: retained only for compatibility.

Runtime permissions, approval records, and audit evidence are future platform responsibilities, not frontend fixture fields.
